Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 5, 2026

1. Who We Are

gen.aditor.ai ("Service") is operated by Aditor Inc. ("we", "us", "our"). We provide AI-powered creative production workflows for agencies and e-commerce brands.

Contact: player@aditor.ai

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Name, email address, and password (hashed) when you create an account
  • Billing information processed securely by Stripe (we never store full card details)
  • Subscription plan and usage data

Content You Upload

  • Product URLs submitted for scraping
  • Images uploaded for workflows (inspiration images, product screenshots, competitor ads)
  • Scripts and text submitted for analysis

Automatically Collected

  • IP address, browser type, and device information
  • Workflow usage patterns (which workflows you use, how often)
  • Error logs for debugging

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Service delivery: Running your workflows and generating creative assets
  • Account management: Authentication, billing, and subscription management
  • Improvement: Understanding usage patterns to improve workflows and features
  • Communication: Sending account-related emails (billing, security, feature updates)
  • Support: Responding to your questions and troubleshooting issues

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate:

  • Stripe — Payment processing (privacy policy)
  • VAP Media / AI Providers — Image and video generation (content processed per request, not stored by providers)
  • Google Cloud Platform — Infrastructure hosting

We do not sell your data to advertisers or data brokers. Ever.

5. Content Ownership

Your content is yours. Images, videos, and creative assets generated through our workflows belong to you. We claim no ownership over your generated content.

We may temporarily store uploaded content to process your workflows. Content is automatically deleted from our processing servers within 24 hours after generation completes.

6. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained while your account is active. Deleted within 30 days of account closure.
  • Generated content: Available for download for 7 days, then automatically purged.
  • Usage logs: Retained for 90 days for analytics, then anonymized.
  • Billing records: Retained for 7 years per legal requirements.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data in a standard format
  • Object to data processing for marketing purposes

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at player@aditor.ai.

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt
  • All connections encrypted via HTTPS/TLS
  • JWT tokens with secure expiration for session management
  • Payment data handled exclusively by Stripe (PCI DSS compliant)

9. Cookies

We use minimal cookies:

  • Authentication token: Required for login sessions
  • Preferences: Theme and workflow settings

We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated via email to active account holders. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Email us at player@aditor.ai.
